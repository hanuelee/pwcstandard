Offcanvas Navigation Bar

Make navigation bar menus appear from side of the page on small screens.

Important Both the navigation bar and the sidebar must be children of .pwc-offcanvas-nav-wrapper and all content that should slide with the sidebar on small screens must be children of .pwc-offcanvas-content-wrapper.

Important JavaScript must be enabled.

Tip When using left offcanvas navigation bar, make sure that parent of .pwc-offcanvas-nav-wrapper and .pwc-offcanvas-content-wrapper has property overflow-x: hidden to prevent horizontal scrolling.
